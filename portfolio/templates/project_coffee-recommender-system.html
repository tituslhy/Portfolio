BaristaBoard is created by a group of coffee enthusiasts who found a need for recommending coffee to fellow Singaporeans. We scrape reviews from Yelp and analyze cafe ratings, textual reviews, images, date posted and geo-location of each review before making a recommendation. Reviews include local favorites (kopi) and artisanal flavors (flat white, mocha, etc.). Recommendation models developed include matrix factorization, collaborative matrix factorization, tensor factorization, and multi-modal recommendation models.

Technologies used

  • Tensorflow
  • Cornac
  • Networkx
  • Plotly
  • Spacy
  • Hyperopt
