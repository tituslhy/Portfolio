This project experiments with generating song lyrics using various deep learning architectures including GPT-2, trained on a corpus of Singapore patriotic songs. We then used Jukebox, a pre-trained AI model developed by OpenAI to generate music and vocals from these lyrics. The final melodies and vocals were not amazing though so we had to sing the AI-generated song lyrics ourselves. At that time, GPT-2 was absolutely state-of-the-art so we thought that it will be quite awhile before AI is able to beat human music celebrities. With the advent of ChatGPT, the wait might be a very short one.

Technologies used

  • Tensorflow
  • GPT-2
  • Jukebox
  • NLTK
  • Vader
